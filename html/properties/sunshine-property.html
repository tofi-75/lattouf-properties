T3 Sunshine

Our second project, T3 Sunshine, which consists of 4 floors and 8 apartments, was successfully completed and handed over to the owners in May 2022. This uniquely designed property, located near the Marina, was fully sold out and delivered on schedule, meeting all our tenants' expectations. The prime location of T3 Sunshine has provided renters with a high return on investment. Additionally, the property is equipped with a solar system, and the process of issuing title deeds is currently in progress.
