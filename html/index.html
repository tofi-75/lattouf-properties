About Us

Welcome to Lattouf Properties Ltd, your gateway to a comfortable home in Cyprus. Established in 2017, we specialize in helping interested clients find their ideal overseas property, seamlessly facilitating the process of buying, selling, and applying for residency.

Why Lattouf Properties?
  • Expert Guidance: With an experience of over 10 years in the Cypriot market, we offer expert guidance tailored to the unique needs of Lebanese citizens looking to invest overseas.
  • Tailored Solutions: Our curated portfolio features properties that promise comfort, luxury, and convenience, ensuring you find the right home for you.
  • Residency Support: Beyond finding your perfect property, we assist with the residency application process, making your transition as smooth as possible.
