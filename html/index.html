About Us

Welcome to Lattouf Properties Ltd, your gateway to a comfortable home in Cyprus. Established in 2021, we specialize in helping Lebanese citizens find their ideal overseas property, seamlessly facilitating the process of buying, selling, and applying for residency.

Why Lattouf Properties?
  • Expert Guidance: With deep roots in both Lebanon and Cyprus, we offer expert guidance tailored to the unique needs of Lebanese citizens looking to invest in Cyprus.
  • Tailored Solutions: Our curated portfolio features properties that promise comfort, luxury, and convenience, ensuring you find the right home for you.
  • Residency Support: Beyond finding your perfect property, we assist with the residency application process, making your transition as smooth as possible.
